ReForm50 – 7 Day Challenge

Move slowly. Rest as needed. Only go as far as you can.
If anything feels painful, dizzy, or unsafe, stop.
Chair Sit to Stand

Reps: 6–10 slow repetitions.
Feet flat. Stand up slowly. Sit back down with control.
Use hands if needed. Only go as far as you can.

Heel Raises

Reps: 8–12 slow repetitions.
Hold a chair or wall for balance. Lift gently. Lower with control.
Only lift as high as feels safe.

Wall Push Ups

Reps: 6–10 slow repetitions.
Hands on wall. Body straight. Bend elbows slowly, then press back.
Step closer or farther to adjust. Only go as far as you can.

Cat Cow Stretch

Reps: 6–8 slow rounds.
Move gently between rounding and relaxing your spine.
Move only within comfort. Only go as far as you can.

Bridge

Reps: 8–10 slow repetitions.
Feet close to hips. Lift hips gently. Lower slowly.
Only go as far as you can.

Safety note

These movements are gentle and optional. This is not medical advice.
If you have a medical condition or concerns, speak with a healthcare provider before starting.
Stop if you feel pain, dizziness, or discomfort. You are always in control.
