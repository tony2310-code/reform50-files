ReForm50 – 7-Day Gentle Movement Challenge

Important Disclaimer & Permission to Proceed

This program is for educational purposes only. You are responsible for your own safety. Only perform movements that feel safe and comfortable for you. Move slowly. Stop immediately if you feel pain, dizziness, or discomfort. By continuing, you acknowledge that you are choosing to participate at your own pace and within your personal limits.

Bridge

Reps: 6–10 slow reps
Lift hips gently. Pause briefly. Lower slowly.
Only go as far as you comfortably can.

Cat–Cow Stretch

Reps: 6–10 slow rounds
Move with your breath.
Only go as far as you comfortably can.

Chair Sit-to-Stand

Reps: 5–8 reps
Use the chair for support.
Only go as far as you comfortably can.

Heel Raises

Reps: 8–12 slow reps
Hold a wall or chair if needed.
Only go as far as you comfortably can.

Wall Push-Ups

Reps: 6–10 reps
Stand close to the wall. Move slowly.
Only go as far as you comfortably can.
