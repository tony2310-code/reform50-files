ReForm50 – 7 Day Movement Challenge

Before you begin:

These movements are gentle and optional.
This is not medical advice.
Listen to your body at all times.

If you feel pain, dizziness, or discomfort, stop.
Only move in ways that feel safe for you.

If you have a medical condition or concerns, speak with your healthcare provider before starting.

You are always in control.
Only go as far as you can.

Bridge

Lie on your back with knees bent and feet on the floor.
Lift your hips gently, then lower slowly.
Move at a comfortable pace.

Reps: 8–10 slow repetitions.
Only go as far as you can.

Cat–Cow Stretch

Move slowly between rounding and relaxing your spine.
Let your breath guide the movement.

Reps: 6–8 slow rounds.
Only go as far as you can.

Chair Sit to Stand

Sit tall in a chair.
Stand up using your legs, then sit back down with control.

Reps: 6–10 repetitions.
Use hands if needed. Only go as far as you can.

Heel Raises

Hold onto a chair or wall for balance.
Lift your heels, then lower slowly.

Reps: 8–12 repetitions.
Only lift as high as feels safe.

Wall Push Ups

Stand facing a wall with hands on the wall.
Bend your arms slowly, then press back.

Reps: 6–10 repetitions.
Step closer or farther to adjust. Only go as far as you can.
