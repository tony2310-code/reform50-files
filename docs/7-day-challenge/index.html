ReForm50 – 7 Day Challenge Exercise Videos

Important Safety Notice

These exercises are gentle and designed for beginners. Move slowly and only within a pain-free range. Stop immediately if you feel sharp pain, dizziness, or discomfort. This program is not medical advice. You are always in control of how much you do.

1. Chair Sit To Stand

Purpose: Helps you safely practice standing up and sitting down, which is something you do every day.

One full rep looks like this:

  • Sit near the front of a sturdy chair.
  • Place both feet flat on the floor, about hip-width apart.
  • Lean your chest slightly forward.
  • Push through your heels.
  • Slowly stand up until you are fully upright.
  • Pause for one second while standing.
  • Slowly lower yourself back down into the chair.
  • Once seated, that is one full repetition.

Breathing: Exhale as you stand up. Inhale as you sit down.

Reps: 5 to 10 slow repetitions.

Important: Use your hands on the chair if needed. Only go as far as you comfortably can.

2. Wall Push Ups

Purpose: Strengthens arms and shoulders without getting on the floor.

One full rep looks like this:

  • Stand facing a wall.
  • Place your hands on the wall at shoulder height.
  • Step your feet slightly back.
  • Keep your body straight.
  • Bend your elbows and slowly lean your chest toward the wall.
  • Stop when your elbows are bent comfortably.
  • Push yourself away from the wall.
  • Return to the starting position. That is one rep.

Breathing: Inhale as you lean in. Exhale as you push away.

Reps: 5 to 10 repetitions.

Important: Move slowly. Only lean in as far as feels safe.

3. Bridge

Purpose: Gently strengthens hips and supports your lower back.

One full rep looks like this:

  • Lie on your back with knees bent.
  • Feet flat on the floor, close to your hips.
  • Arms relaxed by your sides.
  • Press your feet into the floor.
  • Slowly lift your hips upward.
  • Lift only as high as feels comfortable.
  • Pause briefly at the top.
  • Slowly lower your hips back to the floor.

Breathing: Exhale as you lift. Inhale as you lower.

Reps: 5 to 10 repetitions.

Important: Do not force height. Comfort comes first.

4. Cat Cow Stretch

Purpose: Gently moves the spine and reduces stiffness.

One full cycle looks like this:

  • Start on hands and knees.
  • Hands under shoulders. Knees under hips.
  • Inhale and gently arch your back.
  • Lift your chest slightly. This is Cow.
  • Exhale and gently round your back.
  • Tuck your chin slightly. This is Cat.

Breathing: Move slowly with your breath.

Reps: 5 to 8 slow cycles.

Important: This is a stretch, not a workout. Gentle only.

5. Heel Raises

Purpose: Strengthens calves and helps with balance.

One full rep looks like this:

  • Stand tall near a wall or chair.
  • Place your hands lightly on support.
  • Feet flat and hip-width apart.
  • Slowly lift both heels off the floor.
  • Rise onto the balls of your feet.
  • Pause briefly at the top.
  • Slowly lower heels back down.

Breathing: Breathe normally.

Reps: 8 to 12 repetitions.

Important: Hold support if needed. Balance matters more than height.
